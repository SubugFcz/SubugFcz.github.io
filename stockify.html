    STOCKIFY
    TECHNICAL ANALYSIS RANKING FOR STOCK

    Hacklytics 2023

    Nathan Leo
    Rafy Akbar
    Robert Raharjo
    Ignatius Djaynurdin

    The Hacklytics Hackathon Project is a stock analysis and prediction tool that employs technical analysis to rate and rank companies based on their investment potential. The project's objective is to offer investors a comprehensive and impartial evaluation of potential investment opportunities.

    The tool utilizes a blend of Python scripts and libraries to collect, analyze, and visualize data concerning the performance of individual stocks. It takes into account a range of factors, including stock price and volume, to produce a composite score for each company. Subsequently, the companies are ranked according to their scores, with the highest rank indicating the company most likely to yield a favorable return on investment.
